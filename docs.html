Frequently Asked Questions

Questions degens actually ask.

Is this financial advice? ‚ñº
No. Absolutely not. Nega-AI is a meme-speaking language model. Nothing on this site is financial advice. If you lose money please do not @ us on Twitter.
Why can't I see my token? ‚ñº
Your token might not be indexed by DexScreener yet (can take a few minutes after launch), or the CA might be wrong. Double check the mint address and try again ser.
Why does it say "swap not available"? ‚ñº
Jupiter doesn't support all tokens. Tokens with very low liquidity, new tokens with no routing, or tokens on unsupported DEXes won't have a swap route. Try again later or use pump.fun directly.
Is my OpenAI key safe? ‚ñº
Your key is stored in your browser's localStorage only and is sent directly to api.openai.com. We never see it, log it, or store it on our servers. You can verify this in the browser DevTools network tab.
Can I get a refund on my 0.03 SOL listing fee? ‚ñº
No. It's a blockchain transaction. Once confirmed it's gone. This is crypto ser, you know how it works.
Why is there a fried chicken cursor? ‚ñº
Because we thought it was funny. That's it. That's the whole reason.
